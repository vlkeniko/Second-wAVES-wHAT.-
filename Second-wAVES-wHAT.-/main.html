Instagram


The purpose of this platform

This is the main social media platform, and here we want to share content that is interesting and helpul, content that is also funny and relatable to the target audience. We will also make stories every day, so that the instagram page becomes part of the followers life in a way. In stories we will remind followers about upcoming events, show them some user generated content by sharing their stories, and interact with them with polls, votes and Q&A. The main goal is to build a community.

Tone of voice of BAAA

How do we want to approach them(Our target audience)?

We want to communicate in a trustable, reliable and friendly way to our target audience. In our informative content we don’t want to sound like parents nagging their children about doing something. We want to be more approachable, and cool.

Dimensions

  • Funny vs. Serious

In our Instagram, we want to be funny, to create relatable, share-worthy content and more user engagement, in a way that our target audience will still trust us and take us seriously. In our facebook page we want to be more serious, focusing on events, and informative content.

  • Formal vs. Casual

We chose to speak in an informal, personal tone but still sound reliableand not too cringy.

  • Respectful vs. Irreverent

We are respectful, because we want to show how we care about students well being and also inclusivity.

  • Enthusiastic vs. Matter-of-fact

We want to be more enthusiastic, to make sure that the new students will be excited about the upcoming events at the academy. We want to show this enthusiasm mostly in our instagram stories.

Target Audience

Our target audience is international students between the age of 18 - 30, who are considering to apply for the Multimedia courses beginning in September. We start to target our content to them when they first consider applying to BAAA Multimedia courses and then guiding them through their first academic year.

The one big thing that connects the people who we are targeting, that they have to make a big decision about where to study. It is really challenging to be a young international student, alone in a foregin country first time in your life. Our goal is to became a reliable source of information, and also to make them choose this institution for their future studies.
