Year plan

Tone of voice of BAAA

How do we want to approach them(Our target audience)?

We want to communicate in a trustable, reliable and friendly way to our target audience. In our informative content we don’t want to sound like parents nagging their children about doing something. We want to be more approachable, and cool.

Dimensions

  • Funny vs. Serious

In our Instagram, we want to be funny, to create relatable, share-worthy content and more user engagement, in a way that our target audience will still trust us and take us seriously. In our facebook page we want to be more serious, focusing on events, and informative content.

  • Formal vs. Casual

We chose to speak in an informal, personal tone but still sound reliableand not too cringy.

  • Respectful vs. Irreverent

We are respectful, because we want to show how we care about students well being and also inclusivity.

  • Enthusiastic vs. Matter-of-fact

We want to be more enthusiastic, to make sure that the new students will be excited about the upcoming events at the academy. We want to show this enthusiasm mostly in our instagram stories.
